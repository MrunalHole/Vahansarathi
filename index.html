A closer look at your vehicle's voyage

Overview

The system is designed to develop vehicle tracking system with advanced features. It will provide the new way to keep eye on vehicles.  Fingerprint verification of driver/ user will provide the anti-theft security to the vehicle.

Tracking

Personal as well as commercial vehicles can be tracked and located by our software easily.Different vehicles can be tracked simultaneously with accurate measures.

Parental Control

Nowadays, the security of the children is the prime concern for the parent . So , we are enriching our system with the parental control feature in which they can track the exact location of their child's vehicle.

Security 

For the security purposes, we have enabled our system with the feature of fingerprint scanners where the engine will only start unless and untill the fingerprint scans matches with the authorised user.

Effective Tracking of Vehicles

The vehicle tracking system will keep tracking the live location of the vehicle even if the vehicle isn't running. For live location, we will use embedded system in which GPS module will be used for live location and latest 4G/LTE modules will be used to provide internet service to the system. All these modules will be integrated with raspberry pi. Also, the system will keep records of journey and therefore the routes for further analysis. The anti-theft security will provide facilities like driver of vehicle are going to be verified by his/her fingerprints.
